About the Breed

Origin

Kangal Shepherd is often referred to as a sheep dog or flock guardian that can defend against predators of all sizes. Typically, in the Sivas Mountains of Turkey, they are known to successfully protect against wolves, bear and jackals. They have also been transported to other countries, like Africa, to help protect tribes against lions, cheetahs, and other big cats.
